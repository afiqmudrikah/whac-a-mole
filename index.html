Instructions

  • Click the 'START' button to begin the game.
  • Moles will appear randomly from the holes. Use your mouse or finger to click on the moles to hit them before they start hiding.
  • Score points for every successful hit.
  • If you click on an empty hole, you will lose points.
  • Keep an eye on the timer.
  • Game ends when the time limit is up and your score will be displayed.
